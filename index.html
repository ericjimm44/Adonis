N 02 — The protocol

Train like the villain
looked like the god.

The Killmonger build came from high-volume hypertrophy work, relentless supersets, short rests and fighter conditioning. This app encodes those principles into every session it writes for you.

I

Volume is the language

10–15 rep hypertrophy ranges, multiple angles per muscle, chasing the pump — not one-rep maxes.

II

Supersets, short rests

Paired movements back to back, 30–60 seconds between rounds. Density does the sculpting.

III

The Adonis ratio

Shoulders and lats get priority every week. Width up top, tight through the waist — that's the silhouette.

IV

A fighter's engine

Boxing rounds, intervals and core circuits keep you lean enough for the detail to show.
